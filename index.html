Directions

When clicking on boxes, numbers inside those boxes will appear. These numbers indicate how many bombs are near that square(1-8).

However, you don't want to click those bombs so you need to right click(two finger click for laptop gang) to place a flag which will prevent you from tapping the bomb square and loosing. When you get towards the end of the game, all squares that you think are bombs will need to be flagged in order to win.

On the common chance that you do click the square with a bomb, all squares with bombs will be lit up and you will subsequently recieve a loser notice (take the L).
